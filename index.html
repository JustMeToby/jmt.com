Painting

When I'm not working, or reading, or watching tv, I sometimes paint. It all started, when at the tender age of two, I knocked over a glass of water onto a fountain pen resting on a piece of paper.

A few years ago I started doing action paintings, which is basically flinging paint randomly around a canvas using a wide variety of tools. It's surprisingly therapeutic.

You can take a look at some of my paintings at frazzledpainter.com
